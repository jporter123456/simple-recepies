Garlic-Parmesan Green Beans

Air Fryer: 375°F – 8 min | Instant Pot: 0 min + QR | Oven: 400°F – 18 min

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups green beans (trimmed)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp grated parmesan (optional)
  • Salt & pepper

Instructions:

  1. Toss beans with oil, garlic, salt & pepper.
  2. Top with parmesan if using.
  3. Cook per method until tender, slightly crisp.

Tofu Scramble Breakfast Wrap
