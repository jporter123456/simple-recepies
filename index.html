Crispy Tofu Bites

Air Fryer: 400°F, 10 min | Instant Pot: N/A | Oven: 425°F, 20 min

Ingredients:

  • 1 block firm tofu
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Press tofu to remove water, cut into cubes.
  2. Toss with soy sauce, cornstarch, and oil.
  3. Cook in chosen appliance until crispy.
