Garlic-Parmesan Green Beans

Air Fryer: 375°F – 8 min | Instant Pot: 0 min + Quick Release | Oven: 400°F – 18 min

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups green beans (trimmed)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp grated parmesan (optional for vegan)
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Toss green beans with olive oil, garlic, salt, and pepper.
  2. Sprinkle with parmesan if using.
  3. Cook using preferred method until tender and slightly crisp.
