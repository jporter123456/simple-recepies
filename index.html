Garlic-Parmesan Green Beans

Air Fryer: 375°F – 8 min | Instant Pot: 0 min + Quick Release | Oven: 400°F – 18 min

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups green beans (trimmed)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp grated parmesan (optional for vegan)
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Toss green beans with olive oil, garlic, salt, and pepper.
  2. Sprinkle with parmesan if using.
  3. Cook using preferred method until tender and slightly crisp.

Tofu Scramble Breakfast Wrap

Stovetop: Med heat – 10 min | Air Fryer (optional to crisp): 375°F – 4 min

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 block firm tofu
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp black salt
  • 1/4 cup diced peppers
  • 1 wrap (gluten-free optional)

Instructions:

  1. Crumble tofu; mix with spices and peppers.
  2. Cook on stovetop until golden.
  3. Fill wrap; air fry to crisp if desired.

Greek Yogurt Egg Salad

Stovetop: Boil eggs – 10 min | Air Fryer (no water): 270°F – 15 min

Ingredients:

  • 4 boiled eggs
  • 2 tbsp plain Greek yogurt
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1/2 tsp dried dill
  • Salt & pepper

Instructions:

  1. Peel and chop eggs.
  2. Mix with yogurt, mustard, dill, salt & pepper.
  3. Serve in wraps, bowls, or on toast.
